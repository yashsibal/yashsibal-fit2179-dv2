# Data folder

This folder contains cleaned CSV files extracted from the official AusPlay and ABS Excel workbooks.

## Important interpretation note

Participation rates by individual activity are not mutually exclusive. A single adult can participate in more than one activity, so rates should not be added together as if they represent unique people.

## Files

- `top_activities.csv`: Top adult activities by participation rate.
- `sport_non_sport_overall.csv`: AusPlay sport-related / non-sport-related participation split.
- `sport_non_sport_by_age.csv`: Sport/non-sport participation split by age group.
- `flexible_activities.csv`: Selected flexible lifestyle/fitness activities.
- `traditional_sports.csv`: Selected traditional sport activities.
- `activity_by_age.csv`: Selected activities by age group for the heatmap.
- `state_participation.csv`: Adult participation rate and estimated adult participants by state/territory.
- `state_population_participation.csv`: State/territory population from ABS combined with AusPlay participation rates.
- `gender_by_activity.csv`: Male and female participation rates by selected activity.
- `age_profiles.csv`: Age profiles for small multiples.
- `sport_fitness_spectrum.csv`: Derived storytelling classification for the custom spectrum chart.
- `data_sources.csv`: Source details and usage notes.
